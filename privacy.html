Emfy

Privacy Policy

Effective 8 July 2026

Emfy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It has no analytics, no accounts, and no ability to make network connections.

What Emfy collects

Nothing. Emfy contains no analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, advertising identifiers, or usage tracking of any kind, and it never asks you to create an account.

Your files

EMF files you open, preview, or export are processed entirely on your Mac. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared. The app and its Quick Look extensions run sandboxed and are built without a network entitlement — the operating system prevents them from making network connections at all.

Quick Look previews and thumbnails

When Finder shows a preview or thumbnail of an .emf file, macOS invokes Emfy's sandboxed extensions on your Mac. The rendering happens locally and the file's contents never leave your device.

Third-party services

Emfy uses no third-party services, SDKs, or dependencies. There is nothing in the app that could share data with anyone.

App Store distribution

If you install Emfy from the Mac App Store, Apple may collect standard download and diagnostics information according to your device settings and Apple's privacy policy. That collection is controlled by Apple, not by Emfy.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
